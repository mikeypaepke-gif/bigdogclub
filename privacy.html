Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Big Dog Club ("we", "us", "our") operates patrickbig.dog and bigdogclub.vip. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our sites and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with TikTok, we receive the following information from TikTok's API:

  • Your TikTok display name
  • Your TikTok open ID (a unique identifier assigned by TikTok)
  • Your TikTok profile picture URL

We do not receive your TikTok password, private messages, or financial information.

We also collect NFC tap events when you tap Patrick merch — specifically the item type and a local tap count stored on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify you as a Big Dog Club member
  • To personalize your experience (e.g., display your name on the welcome page)
  • To track engagement with NFC-enabled merch (tap streaks, milestone rewards)
  • To notify you about exclusive drops and giveaways

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. TikTok Integration

We use TikTok's Login Kit to authenticate members. By signing in with TikTok, you agree to TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

If you have granted the auto-follow permission, we will request a follow of @patrickbigdog and @bigdogclub on your behalf. You can unfollow at any time from within TikTok.

4. Cookies & Storage

We use a single session cookie (bdc_session) to keep you logged in for up to 30 days. NFC tap counts are stored locally on your device using localStorage — this data never leaves your device.

5. Data Retention

Session data is stored only for the duration of your login session (30 days). We do not maintain a permanent server-side database of member profiles at this time.

6. Your Rights

You can revoke Big Dog Club's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's app: Settings → Privacy → Apps and Websites. This will end your Big Dog Club session on your next visit.

7. Children's Privacy

Big Dog Club is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at privacy@bigdogclub.vip.
